17 Royal Duties of King Rama X

The important royal duties of His Majesty King Maha Vajiralongkorn Bodindradebayavarangkun, King Rama X, the king are full of genius and talent in studying, military, sports, international relations, and others. He devoted his body to performing many royal duties.

Since he was young, His Majesty started traveling to various regions. His Majesty looked at the life, both happiness and sorrow, of the inhabitant. To learn guidelines for granting royal assistance, including the development of careers, the development of water sources, and the lives of poor people in remote areas. Because of this, we have provided 10 royal duties of King Rama X as follow.



1. Education

His Majesty King Maha Vajiralongkorn Bodindradebayavarangkun graciously donated royal funds to support the Department of General Education and Ministry of Education, established six secondary schools in remote areas, and also received the schools under royal patronage. His Majesty bestowed modern educational materials such as computers, televisions, videos, and in the field of higher education.



2. Medical and Public Health

His Majesty Maha Vajiralongkorn Bodindradebayavarangkun is aware that the good health and wellbeing of the people are important factors in the creation of human resources. Therefore, His Majesty is interested in performing royal duties, such as when the government graciously offered twenty one Somdej Phra Yupparat hospitals throughout the country. His Majesty made an effort to come to perform the opening ceremony of every hospital and visited the hospitals regularly, including granting royal funds to support medical equipment and modern tools.



3. Social Work

His Majesty Maha Vajiralongkorn Bodindradebayavarangkun cared about improving the quality of people's lives, especially those of the underprivileged. His Majesty visited many slum communities in Bangkok, such as the Phra Khanong slum community, Khlong Toei District, and Yannawa District, and donated royal funds to support community projects such as the project to develop underprivileged children and the drug suppression project.



4. Foreign Affairs

His Majesty traveled on behalf of His Majesty King Bhumibol Adulyadej and Her Majesty Queen Suthida Bajrasudhabimalalakshana to visit friendly countries around the continent, such as Italy, the Republic of China, Japan, Iran, Nepal, the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, the Republic of Peru, Australia, and Singapore. In addition to focusing on developing friendly relations, His Majesty was also interested in observing and studying various activities that His Majesty will use to develop Thailand, such as visiting military affairs, arts and culture, industry, and the well-being of the people.



5. Agriculture

His Majesty King Maha Vajiralongkorn Bodindradebayavarangkun, when he was still the Crown Prince Maha Vajiralongkorn, traveled and proceeded to make compost from water hyacinth and other weeds as an auspicious occasion to give to farmers for use in cultivation to increase productivity at Ban Laem Sakae, Doem Bang Subdistrict, Doem Bang Nang Buat District, and Suphanburi Province. On March 23, 1985, In addition, His Majesty performed royal duties to promote agricultural affairs, such as representing His Majesty in the Royal Plowing Ceremony.



6. Religion

His Majesty traveled on behalf of His Majesty to perform religious duties on a regular basis, such as changing the clothing of the Buddha Maha Mani Rattana Patimakorn at Wat Phra Sri Rattana Satsadaram according to the season, as well as accompanying His Majesty to the presentation of trophies and the National Test of Invoking the Holy Qur'an.



7. Sports

His Majesty granted royal permission to organize a cycling activity “Bike Un Ai Rak” at the event “Un Ai Rak, Relieve Cold, the Waters of Rattanakosin," gave shirts for cycling and royal drinking water to the people who participated in the activity, and led people to ride bicycles on the "Bike Un Ai Rak" route from Phra Lan, Dusit Palace, to Lat Pho Health Park, Phra Pradaeng District, Samut Prakan Province, with a total round trip distance of 39 km. It is scheduled to take place at 3 p.m. on December 9, 2018. This activity was allowed by the King to be held in order to show that the monarchy and the people are close together. In addition, those who participated in the cycling activity also showed the unity of the Thai people throughout the nation and saw the importance of exercising and promoting good physical health.



8. Military

His Majesty has been interested in military technology since he was young. In addition to receiving military education from Australia, he was also diligent in increasing his knowledge and experience all the time. Especially in the field of aviation science. He has served in the military since January 9, 1975, and has held the military rank of 3 armed forces, namely General, Admiral, and Air Chief Marshal, participating in combating terrorism in the North and Northeast. Including protecting the area around the Cambodian refugee camp at Khao Lan, Trat Province, he also attended military ceremonies such as the Ratchawallop Day ceremony.



9. Aviation

In 2009, His Majesty served as the first pilot of a Boeing 737-400 on the Family Bond flight. Help flood victims and provide medical equipment for hospitals in the 3 southern border provinces, flight TG 8870 (Bangkok to Chiang Mai) and flight TG 8871 (Chiang Mai to Bangkok).



10. Goverment Service

From January 9, 1992, to the present, he holds the position of Commander of the Security Dedication Command. The Office of the Supreme Commander is currently the Command for His Majesty's Security and Protection. It is a government agency of His Majesty.



11. Mobile Agricultural Clinic

Mobile agricultural clinic under the patronage of the Crown Prince Maha Vajiralongkorn, Crown Prince of Thailand. It is a project that helps increase productivity, create motivation, and stimulate the agricultural production economy by providing academic services and transferring appropriate technology or innovation. This is an important factor for the development of agriculture in the form of integrating academics in each field, including plants, livestock, fisheries, land development, etc., operating since 2002 until the present.



12. Kasetwichaya Project

His Majesty King Maha Vajiralongkorn when he held the royal title of Crown Prince, His Majesty granted royal land in the area of Ban Kong Hae Garden, Village No. 4, Pong Yaeng Subdistrict, Mae Rim District, Chiang Mai Province, amounting to 1,350 rai to the Ministry of Agriculture and Cooperatives to operate as an agricultural clinic to disseminate research results and agricultural technology from the Huai Hong Khrai Development Study Center under the royal initiative in the form of a community technology service and transfer center as a training and research center for appropriate agricultural development with the area, conserve soil, water and forest resources to maintain their natural perfection sustainably.



13. The Royal Ploughing Ceremony

His Majesty King Maha Vajiralongkorn, when he held the royal title of Crown Prince, was the representative of His Majesty King Bhumibol Adulyadej the Great (King Rama IX), who has presided over the Royal Plowing Ceremony of the Royal Palace for more than 10 years. He presided over the Royal Ploughing Ceremony on May 12, 2017 until the present in response to His Majesty the King. His Majesty's determination continues.



14. Project of Demonstration Farms to Fight COVID-19

His Majesty King Vajiralongkorn has a royal determination to continue, maintain, and expand, so he has granted royal permission to use the land in the Model Farm Project under His Majesty King Bhumibol Adulyadej The Great, all 30 farms within 17 provinces out of 61 farms nationwide to be used to support employment under “Project of Demonstration Farms to Fight COVID-19” by embracing the new agricultural theory project of His Majesty King Bhumibol Adulyadej The Great. Made the model farm a source of safe food production, income, and sustainable occupation without people having to leave their homes and to be an example of sustainable success forever.



15. Khok Nong Na Model

His Majesty King Vajiralongkorn performed a ceremony to worship in the Khok Nong Na areas in the area of Ruan Ton Palace, Wimanmek Palace, and Dusit Palace. His Majesty the King gave a speech on “Ariyakaset” on January 29, 2021, saying, “Kok Nong Na, besides being a symbol of agricultural abundance, is also an art. It's a good exercise. In order to bring together various types of agriculture in the same area at the same time, maintain variety while ensuring that diversity supports each other.



16. Project to develop the Nong Ung area, under the royal initiative of King Rama X, changing the lives of the Kho Nuea people

“Area development project in Nong Ung area due to the royal initiative Yasothon Province," Kho Nuea Subdistrict, Mueang District, Yasothon Province, is one of many royal projects initiated by His Majesty King Maha Vajiralongkorn Bodindradebayavarangkun. He cares about the people and gave the idea to solve the agricultural problems of the people who faced the problems of repeated flooding of their fields in the rainy season and extreme drought at the end of the season.



17. Royal Scholarship Project

His Majesty the King, when he held the royal title of Crown Prince, initiated a royal initiative to proceed with “The Crown Prince Maha Vajiravudh Scholarship Project," established in 2009, allowing the royal family's personal wealth and donations to be used as royal merit. His Majesty's determination to create stable educational opportunities for Thai youths who behave well, have the ability to study, and are given the opportunity to study at the high school level continuously until completing a bachelor's degree according to each person's abilities. Which is an investment to develop the knowledge, abilities, and potential of Thai youth.
